Helpful Safety Tips for Everyone

Lock Your Doors and Windows

Ensure all doors and windows are securely locked before leaving home or going to bed. Simple precautions help prevent break-ins.

Stay Alert in Public Spaces

Be aware of your surroundings at all times. Avoid distractions such as your phone when walking in unfamiliar areas, especially at night.

Report Suspicious Activity

If you notice anything out of the ordinary or feel unsafe, report it to local authorities immediately. Your vigilance can help prevent crime.

Use Well-Lit Pathways at Night

When walking or cycling at night, always use well-lit streets. If possible, walk with a friend or use trusted transport options.

Protect Your Online Identity

Be cautious about sharing personal information on social media and always use strong, unique passwords for your accounts.
